Privacy Policy

App name: bot

Last updated: July 2026

What this app does

This is a personal automation tool that uploads videos you provide to your own social media accounts (including TikTok) on a schedule you control.

Information we collect

  • TikTok account authorization tokens (stored locally on your device only)
  • Video files you explicitly queue for upload
  • Upload history stored locally for deduplication

How we use information

Data is used only to authenticate with TikTok and post content you supply. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data storage

All credentials and videos are stored on your own computer or server. No cloud backend is operated by this app.

Your rights

You can revoke app access at any time from your TikTok account settings or by deleting local credential files.
